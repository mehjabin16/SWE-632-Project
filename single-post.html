The Classic Lasagna Recipe

While there are a few steps to this lasagna recipe, it’s easy to make and has so much flavor. This dish can be made ahead of time and freezes well either before or after baking!

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

For the Cheese Filling:

  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Other

  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat. Add chopped onions and garlic to the skillet. Cook until the onions are softened. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, water, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally. In a bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, egg, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper.In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles. Sprinkle a portion of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the ricotta layer. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden. Allow the lasagna to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
